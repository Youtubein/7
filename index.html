BPSC Exam Details and Syllabus

Preliminary Examination Click to Open/Close

General Hindi

  • Subject: General Hindi
  • Marks: 100 marks
  • Number of Questions: 150 questions (MCQ)
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Qualifying Marks: 30% (30 marks) minimum passing marks
  • Details: This paper is qualifying in nature; marks obtained will not be included in the final merit.

General Studies

  • Marks: 150 marks
  • Number of Questions: 150 questions (MCQ)
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Negative Marking: 1/3 marks per wrong answer
  • Details: This paper is screening in nature; marks are not included in the final merit.

Prelims Syllabus

  • General Science: Basic concepts of Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Environment.
  • Current Affairs: National, international, and Bihar-specific events (last 1-2 years).
  • History: Ancient, Medieval, Modern India and Bihar; Freedom Struggle.
  • Geography: Physical, social, and economic aspects of India and Bihar.
  • Polity and Economy: Indian system, Panchayati Raj, changes in Bihar's economy.
  • National Movement: 1857 Revolt, Champaran, Quit India; Bihar's role.
  • Mental Ability: Reasoning, arithmetic, data interpretation.

Main Examination Click to Open/Close

  • Structure:
    1. General Hindi: 100 marks (Qualifying Paper)
    2. General Studies Paper I (GS Paper I): 300 marks
    3. General Studies Paper II (GS Paper II): 300 marks
    4. Essay: 300 marks
    5. Optional Subject: 100 marks (Qualifying Paper)
  • Total Marks: 1000 marks (900 marks for merit)
  • Duration: 3 hours for each descriptive paper, 2 hours for optional paper
  • Qualifying Criteria:
    • General Hindi: 30% (30 marks)
    • Optional Subject:
      • General: 40% (40 marks)
      • Backward Class (BC): 36.5%
      • Extremely Backward Class (EBC): 34%
      • Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe/Women/Disabled: 32%
  • Note: Marks from Essay and GS Papers I and II are included in the merit.

Mains Syllabus

  • General Hindi (100 marks, Qualifying): Essay (30), Grammar (30), Syntax (25), Summarization (15).
  • General Studies Paper 1 (300 marks): Modern history (India and Bihar), Indian culture, current events, statistical analysis.
  • General Studies Paper 2 (300 marks): Indian polity, economy, geography, science and technology.
  • Essay (300 marks): 3 essays (ethical/philosophical, social/economic/political, Bihar-specific).
  • Optional Subject (100 marks, Qualifying): MCQ, choose 1 from 34 subjects (e.g., History, Geography).

Interview Click to Open/Close

  • Tip: Practice mock interviews for confidence.
