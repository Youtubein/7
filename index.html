Prelims Exam Pattern

Single paper (General Studies), 150 MCQs, 150 marks, 2 hours, 1/3 negative marking. Qualifying only.

Syllabus Topics

  • General Science: Basic concepts in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, environment.
  • Current Affairs: National, international, Bihar-specific events (last 1-2 years).
  • History: Ancient, Medieval, Modern India & Bihar; freedom struggle.
  • Geography: Physical, social, economic aspects of India & Bihar.
  • Polity & Economy: Indian system, Panchayati Raj, Bihar economy changes.
  • National Movement: 1857 Revolt, Champaran, Quit India; Bihar’s role.
  • Mental Ability: Reasoning, arithmetic, data interpretation.

Mains Exam Pattern

5 papers, 1000 marks (900 for merit). General Hindi & Optional are qualifying. Descriptive (except Optional: MCQ).

Papers & Syllabus

  • General Hindi (100 marks, Qualifying): Essay (30), Grammar (30), Syntax (25), Precis (15).
  • General Studies Paper 1 (300 marks): Modern History (India & Bihar), Indian Culture, Current Events, Statistical Analysis.
  • General Studies Paper 2 (300 marks): Indian Polity, Economy, Geography, Science & Tech.
  • Essay (300 marks): 3 essays (Moral/Philosophical, Social/Economic/Political, Bihar-specific).
  • Optional Subject (100 marks, Qualifying): MCQ, choose 1 from 34 subjects (e.g., History, Geography).

BPSC Interview Click to Expand/Collapse

Format: Personality test, 120 marks, 20-30 minutes.

Focus: Current affairs, Bihar issues, personal background, ethics.

Weightage: Added to Mains (total 1020 marks).

Tip: Practice mock interviews for confidence.

Study Tips

  • Focus on Bihar-specific topics (history, geography, economy).
  • Read newspapers daily for current affairs (The Hindu, Indian Express).
  • Practice previous years’ papers to understand question trends.
  • Write essays regularly to improve clarity and structure.
  • Use NCERT books for basics; refer to Bihar-specific books (e.g., Imtiyaz Ahmed).
  • Join a test series for Prelims and Mains practice.
  • Revise regularly and make concise notes.
